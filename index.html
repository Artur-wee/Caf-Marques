Our Story

Founded in 2015, Calm Coffee began as a small passion project between two friends who shared a love for quality coffee and tranquil spaces. What started as a modest coffee cart has blossomed into a beloved neighborhood café.

We source our beans directly from ethical growers, roast in small batches, and prepare each drink with meticulous attention to detail. Our baristas are trained in the art of coffee making, ensuring every cup meets our high standards.

More than just a coffee shop, we've created a space where you can pause, breathe, and enjoy the simple pleasure of a perfectly brewed cup.
